Frequently Asked Questions

What are the prerequisites for the Drone Pilot Certification course?

No prior experience is required for our beginner-level Drone Pilot Certification course. However, basic computer skills and comfort with technology are beneficial. Participants should be at least 18 years old.

Do I need to bring my own drone?

No, we provide all necessary equipment including drones, sensors, and software for the duration of the training. However, if you have your own equipment that you'd like to learn on, you're welcome to bring it.

What is the class size for your training programs?

We maintain small class sizes to ensure personalized attention. Our beginner courses have a maximum of 10 participants, while advanced courses are limited to 6 participants.

Do you offer customized corporate training?

Yes, we offer customized training programs tailored to your organization's specific needs. This can include on-site training, specialized curriculum, and flexible scheduling. Contact us to discuss your requirements.

What happens if I need to cancel my registration?

Cancellations made at least 14 days before the course start date receive a full refund. Cancellations between 7-14 days receive a 50% refund. Unfortunately, we cannot provide refunds for cancellations made less than 7 days before the course.
